About ReconLens

ReconLens

v
ReconLens is a professional-grade passive reconnaissance engine designed for modern security research. It automatically extracts, normalizes, and categorizes endpoints, secrets, and intelligence data from web assets in real-time. Featuring a Deep Normalization Engine and AI-Ready Export system, it streamlines the reconnaissance process without making a single active request beyond what the page already loads.
